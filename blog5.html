Artificial Moon on the Earth - Moon World Resorts, Dubai

Dubai is known for its luxury hotels, shopping, and high-end real estate. Some of the most luxurious hotels in Dubai include the Burj Al Arab, the Armani Hotel Dubai, the Ritz-Carlton Dubai, and the Four Seasons Resort Dubai at Jumeirah Beach These hotels offer amenities such as private beaches, spas, and fine dining restaurants. Many of the big investors have been trying t o invest their assets in large new construction.

Moon World Resorts unveil a series of large dome-like hotels in Dubai, which are designed to resemble the moon. According to the company's co-founder Michael Henderson, each dome would be a 360-degree recreation of the moon on a grand scale, complete with realistic-looking structure and texture. Henderson also stated that the idea behind the project is that the moon is a "recognizable brand" and that "seven and a half billion people know it, everybody loves it." However, it's worth noting that this project is still in the planning phase and it's not clear when and if it will be built.



According to Michael R. Henderson, the co-founder of Moon World Resorts, each dome-like hotel will be a luxurious and contemporary destination resort, with various amenities and features, such as a convention centre, restaurants, and spa, all with a lunar theme. The artist renderings of the project envision spaceship-style interiors and themed entertainment, including an experience that replicates walking on the moon. Henderson stated that the company plans to give guests the ability to walk on what they will believe to be the lunar surface. The project has been in the works for two decades and Henderson suggests that it matches the slower pace associated with space tourism more generally. However, it's worth noting that this project is still in the planning phase and it's not clear when and if it will be built.
