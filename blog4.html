Oxagon: Concept of Floating City

Exactly on the Red Sea, located near the Suez Canal, Saudi Arabia is planning to build a floating city named as Oxagon. This floating city will be exactly shaped in an Oxagon shape and will be 500 metre in depth of the Red sea.The project is the idea of one of the leading modern architecture in the world known as NEOM mega project. This futuristic city will be the principle of renewable energy, technology, community, diversity, and modern architecture. The ordinary structure and the idea of this floating city seems imaginary in todayâ€™s times. but, the crown prince of Saudi Arabia claimed that the city targets to house around 90,000 residents by 2030.

As commenced, there will be residential communities, the floating city will considerably have factories, manufacturing facilities and other logistics-related sites. The location of the city will be nearest to the Suez Canal from where 13 percent of the world trade passes, so it will be convenient to Saudi Arabia for trading activities. It will also be proved as one of the advantage factors for the Saudi Arabia industrial sector.

Oxagon will be one of the most unique and modern technologically advanced cities with state of the art integrated port and airport availability. It is designed considering the safety of the ecosystem as well.

Oxagon has planned focusing on following seven factors:

Sustainable Energy, autonomous availability, sustainable food, water innovation, health and wellbeing, technology and advanced manufacturing activities, and trending methods of construction. The advanced architectural design and all those factors will be the pride of the city for the new era.

Big industrial businesses like Gulf Modular International and Hyzon Motors have commenced to set up their workshops in the city. NEOM always comes up with some modern architectural ideas and Oxagon will be one of the collections which will define the artistic ability and technological advancement of Neom, Saudi Arabia.
