3 Steps to know the Purpose and Intent of your Life:

Nowadays, we all are so busy in our own lives. We start our day and end it like we spent yesterday. There is nothing new or innovative. We work, we sleep, we eat, and repeat this cycle for another day. But while doing this all, we just forgot the purpose of our life. How it could be, if you had one checklist of tasks that lead to some purpose. Fortunately, Life needs to always be in transition, which is what makes it so precious. At times, we are just unclear about what purpose we are living for. You spend all your time using mobile phones and searching for new trends, activities, events etc. but, you don't have time to learn yourself, to think about your life purpose. You just go with the flow and it is okay. Nothing is bad in it, but have you some or the other day prioritized yourself and kept all kinds of gadget addiction away from you and just imagined for what purpose god has sent you in this world. Did you bother yourself to know the core purpose of living? Don't worry! Discovering your purpose does not have to be too much difficult- here are five best ways to start your life with some purpose and intent:

1. Find your abilities:

The first step to knowing the purpose of your life is to identify your abilities. What are you passionate about? What can you do in your life? If you don't know the answer to these questions. Just think about the stuff that makes you excited. You have an addiction to mobile gadgets. Right? Check your browsing history and bookmark list for clues. You will find a lot of topics in which you are interested. Just need to claim it!



2. Define your superpower:

Everyone has something unique. You just need to find it out and work on it. Write down what you are good at, choose what you can offer now. You will definitely feel grateful for that skill. Every successful person is aware of their strengths and superpower. They just utilize it in their work and achieve what they want.



3. Be fearless:

Don't be afraid about anything in your life. Time passes on and the circumstances change with the time. Do purposeful things. Don't bother about what others will think. Concentrate on your own work and don't think about the results. That said, Walk like a king or Don't care who is the king. Develop your confidence by performing the right things and you are not so far to find out the purpose and intent of your life. This simple method will definitely help you to make your own path towards success.
